koshari

Koshari is Egypts most iconic and beloved street food, a delicious, carb-heavy comfort meal that is as satisfying as it is unique. It is a harmonious, textured blend of humble staples, traditionally served in a layered bowl and topped with a signature tangy tomato sauce.
